Four Articles, One Design

Editorial Layout

The Goal

The goal of this project was to create a layout that could be consistently used across four articles that are of different topics: fashion, technology, fitness & world news.

The Idea

The inspiration for this design layout came from the New York City based fashion magazine, Nylon. Nylon has more of a cluttered look than I felt would look best for the variety of topics covered in this project. I wanted to make it far more simple since the topics were so broad. I decided to go with clean white pages, with large titles, pull quotes & page numbers. To keep the articles organized, the best choice was to go with colour-coaded sections. I decided to keep the body copy with a large, bold sans-serif for readability while keeping with the modern look of the design.

The Process

To successfully create this design, I referenced Nylon Magazine, while adding my own spin on the look of the layouts, Adobe Photoshop, Adobe InDesign and a Macbook Pro, the magazine quickly came together with hardwork and dedication.
