Pantone Quote Book

Editorial Layout & Typography

About The Project

The objective of this project was to create a quote book with one theme using a combination of our knowledge of type-setting on a computer and creating hand-made typographic pieces. I decided to go with a theme of Pantone Colours of the Year from 2008 to 2015, and people's reactions to the annoucements of the colours. I decided to do the titles as the hand-made typography and type set the quotes from people's reactions to the colours. Each colour name was created with items that resembled or were directly related to the item. I used fake orchid pedals and cut out the words Radiant Orchid, and so on for each colour.
