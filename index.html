A Little Bit About Me.

Hi, my name is Emily! I'm a graphic designer based out of Ottawa with specialization in editorial design, print media & UI/UX design. In addition to these specialties, I also have skills in photography and hand drawn typography. I am proficent in Adobe Illustrator, InDesign, Photoshop, Photoshop Lightroom, and have some knowledge of HTML, CSS & Javascript. I am a passionate animal advocate, and wear black like it's going out of style. If I was in Star Wars, and had to pick between the Rebel Alliance and the Galactic Empire, I'd probably never come up with an answer. I'm a lover of autumn, good kerning, ampersands, Kate Spade, beer and bernese mountain dogs, but not in that particular order. I ramble a lot, and my biography writing is sub-par at best.
